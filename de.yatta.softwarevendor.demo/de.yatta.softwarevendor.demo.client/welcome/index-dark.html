Welcome to the Checkout for Eclipse demo

This demo includes a free sample plugin called Eclipse Intruders to test the Checkout for Eclipse. No real payments or purchases are made in this demo. So, have fun testing—and fight the bugs!

1

Start the demo

Click on the right to launch Eclipse Intruders, including the Checkout for Eclipse demo.

2

Use the Checkout to demo-purchase the game

At first, the game is locked until you get a license (as if the trial expired). To access Eclipse Intruders, create an account (or sign in) and use Yatta Checkout to demo-purchase (one-time or subscription) the game.

3

Complete the demo purchase—and fight the bugs!

Test the Checkout for Eclipse with the provided demo user and sample data for payment & billing. Upon completing the demo purchase, you will get access to the game. Enjoy the streamlined purchase experience of the Checkout—and playing Eclipse Intruders!
