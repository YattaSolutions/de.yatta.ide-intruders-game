Getting started with the Yatta Checkout for Eclipse

With this demo solution you can experience and test the Yatta in-app checkout.
You can subscribe to, purchase or cancel a license. No real transaction fees will be charged.

Start the demo

To start the demo, click on the Software Vendor button on the top left.

How to get a license

The game is locked until you get a license. To do so you can either click "Subscribe" (periodic renewal) or "Purchase" (one time). To continue you will be asked to sign in with your Yatta Account.

Demo billing and payment information

The Checkout has prefilled test data for billing and payment information. You are purchasing the software with test data, therefore no money will be withdrawn from a real bank account. After you click the "Subscribe" or "Purchase" button you will receive a license and can start using it immediately - without entering a license key or leaving your Eclipse IDE.

Managing your solutions

You can manage your licenses in the Yatta Account Management. You can reset your demo license to reset the subscription flow.
