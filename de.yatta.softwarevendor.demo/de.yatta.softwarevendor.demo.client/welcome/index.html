Getting started with the Yatta Checkout for Eclipse

Experience and test the Yatta in-app Checkout using our demo solution.
Test license subscription, purchase and cancellation with no transaction fees.

Starting the demo

Click Software Vendor in the toolbar on the top left.

Getting a license

The game is locked until you get a license. Click Subscribe for a monthly or yearly license, or choose a one-time Purchase. Sign in with your Yatta Account to continue.

Demo billing and payment

The Yatta Checkout has a demo user with test data for billing and payment; no real transactions will take place in the demo. After you click Subscribe or Purchase, you will receive a demo license and can use the demo product immediately â€” without entering a license key or leaving your Eclipse IDE.

Managing your solutions

You can manage your licenses in your Yatta Account. By clicking the three dots on the right, you can edit or reset your demo license to continue testing the purchase flow.
