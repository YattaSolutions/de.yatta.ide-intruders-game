Getting started with the Yatta Checkout for Eclipse

With this demo solution you can experience and test the Yatta in-app checkout.
You can subscribe and cancel a license with the provided test data. No real transaction fees will be charged.

How to get a license

The game is locked until you get a license. To do so you can either click "subscribe" (periodic renewal) or “purchase” (one-time). To continue you will be asked to sign in with your Yatta account.

Demo billing and payment information

The checkout has prefilled test data for billing and payment information. You are purchasing the software with test data, therefore no money will be withdraw from a real bank account. After you press the “Subscribe” or “purchase” button you will receive a climsy bird license and can start using it immediately without entering any license key or leaving your Eclipse IDE.

Managing your solutions

You have the ability to manage your licenses in the Yatta account management. You can reset your demo license to replay the subscription flow.

Start selling your solution

If you want to integrate the check out for your own eclipse solution, you can become a vendor. Once you agreed to our vendor agreement, you will have access to an integration documentation in your account management.
